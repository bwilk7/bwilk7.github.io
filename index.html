I am a PhD Candidate in Computer Science at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County. I work in the Coginition, Robotics, and Learning (CoRaL) lab under the direction of Tim Oates. My current area of research is the lexical semantics on adjectives. Other interests include the use of NLP with endangered languages and reproducible computational science.
